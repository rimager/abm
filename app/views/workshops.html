Arts mean Business Forums

Corporations are looking for a competitive edge and ways to inspire greater innovation from their employees. While, in recent years, much has been written about creativity becoming the currency of the brave new world of worker empowerment and learning at work, and about the need to attract a new creative class to our cities, relatively little attention has been given to ways we might nurture or otherwise invest in local creative capital. The Arts Mean Business Forums expands innovative thinking with networking and featured panel discussions with invited speakers from the corporate and cultural communities.
