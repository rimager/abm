Building a better board workshop

Workshop program for arts executives and board chairs to develop a board recruitment strategy, create board recruitment materials and provide an overview of governing models. At the conclusion of the program arts groups will complete a Board Recruitment Proﬁle. Session includes a board assessment tool to help determine the strengths and weaknesses of the governing board. Participating groups work with a graphic designer and web designer to design their Board Snapshot for the website. This brief proﬁle will provide potential members with an overview of the organizations board commitments and openings. Groups must participate in the workshop and complete their Snapshot to be included on the website.
